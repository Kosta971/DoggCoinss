About DoggCoin

DoggCoin is a fully independent blockchain-based cryptocurrency created from scratch in Python. It offers lightning-fast blocks every 10 seconds and a limited supply of 21,000,000 coins — ensuring scarcity and value.

Key Features

  • 🚀 Own blockchain, not a token
  • 🧱 Block time: 10 seconds
  • 🔒 Secure transaction engine
  • 💰 Total Supply: 21,000,000 DGC
  • 🛠 Built 100% in Python
